# Privacy Policy - GST Calculator India

**Effective Date:** [1 May, 2026]

GST Calculator India ("the App") respects your privacy.

## 1) Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal identity information directly from users.

## 2) Local Data Storage

The App stores calculation history and app preferences (such as theme and number format) locally on your device using local storage. This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

## 3) Network and Sharing

If you use the Share feature, content is shared only through apps/services you choose on your device. We do not receive or store this shared content.

## 4) Advertising

The current version of the App does not display ads.

If ads are enabled in a future update, this policy will be updated to explain ad-related data handling and third-party services used.

## 5) Third-Party Services

The App may use standard platform and framework services (for example, Android/Google Play infrastructure) required for app delivery and operation.

## 6) Data Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, you control it through app/device actions (such as clearing app data or uninstalling the app).

## 7) Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date.
